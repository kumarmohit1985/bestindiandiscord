Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the #1 Indian Discord Community.

What is the most active Discord server in India?

TheIndia is widely recognized as the most active Discord server in India for 2026. With 24/7 active voice chats, thousands of daily messages, and a buzzing community, it is the premier destination for Desi users.

Is there an Indian Discord server for BGMI and Valorant?

Yes! TheIndia features dedicated channels for BGMI scrims, Valorant squads, and Minecraft multiplayer. It is a hub for Indian gamers to find teammates and participate in community tournaments.

How do I find a safe Indian Discord server for girls?

Safety is our priority. TheIndia uses advanced auto-moderation and a 24/7 human staff team to maintain a toxic-free environment, making it one of the safest Indian Discord servers for female gamers and students.

Can I make new friends on Hindi Discord servers?

Absolutely. Most of our members communicate in Hindi and English. We host daily "Chai & Chill" voice sessions and movie watch parties specifically designed to help people make new friends in India.

How do I join an Indian Discord community?

Simply click on an invite link like discord.gg/theindia. Once you join, complete the verification process to unlock all chat and voice channels.

Are there Nitro giveaways on TheIndia?

We frequently host Discord Nitro giveaways, Steam key drops, and special server roles for our most active members and event winners. Check our #giveaways channel regularly!

What are the rules of your Indian community?

Our main rules include: Be respectful to all members, no hate speech or toxicity, no NSFW content, and no spamming. We aim to keep the "Adda" vibe friendly and welcoming for everyone.

Can I promote my stream on your server?

We have a dedicated "Self-Promotion" channel for creators. However, random DM advertising or spamming links in general chat is strictly prohibited to protect our members from spam.

How do I appeal a ban on TheIndia?

If you believe you were unfairly banned, you can reach out to our staff via the Contact Us page on this website or email officialtheindia@gmail.com with your Discord ID and reason for appeal.

Why should I join TheIndia instead of other servers?

Unlike other "dead" servers, TheIndia offers real engagement. Whether it's 3 AM or 3 PM, you will always find someone to talk to, play games with, or share a laugh in our community.
